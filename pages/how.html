HOW IT WORKS

STEP 01

Customer who wants to make a delivery enters product details(he must be logged in)

STEP 02

From his entries, a random code is generated(which the customer has to give to the recipient)

STEP 03

The reicipient then enters the code in the "receive a delivery" section.

STEP 04

Locations of both customer and recipients are determined and linked together.

STEP 05

From the distance and the type of product(s), a priced is determined.

STEP 06

The nearest delivery person is then notified and he comes and pickup the product on payment
